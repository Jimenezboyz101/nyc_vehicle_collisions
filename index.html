# Collisions in the displayed region

This dashboard visualizes vehicle collisions across the city of New York every circle is one collision while the size and color of the circle indicate the severity of the collisions. The chart live updates based on whats currently visible on the users screen. Zoom in and out to see the changes. Click on any vehicle collision to see additional information for the incident. Note some incidents may be stacked on top of each other due to occuring in the same location.
